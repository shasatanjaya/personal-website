Realm of Shadow

Console based-RPG Game

2023 | Game Design - ASCII Animation - C#

Will you rise to the challenge
and outwit the three masters
before their dark plot unfolds?

The idea for Realm of Shadow sparked when my team came across Stone Story, an RPG that creatively used ASCII animations. We were inspired to take on the challenge of building our own console-based RPG, not only as a learning experience, but also to bring a sense of nostalgia and introduce players to a simple yet unique ASCII-driven gameplay style.

My role focused on designing the UI/UX, creating ASCII inspired animations, and ensuring the game flow was smooth and engaging. It was a hands-on creative project that blended coding fundamentals with retro game design.
