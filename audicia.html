Audicia
Audicia was born from a simple question: “What if practicing communication could feel more real and practical?” Inspired by that idea, my team and I developed Audicia, a communication training app that helps users improve their speaking skills through real life scenarios.

This project challenged us to think not just about technical implementation, but also user habits, speaking anxiety, and practical learning. It's not just about speaking better, it's about helping people become more confident communicators in daily life.
