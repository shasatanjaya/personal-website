This AR project was inspired by an experience I had at a church event, where I was introduced to how augmented reality can make interaction more engaging and fun. Motivated by that moment, I decided to create my own AR experience to explore creative expression beyond college and programming.

I personally designed the visual concept, combining my personal interests outside of academics into the AR design. This project allowed me to combine creativity, illustration, and technology, giving me a chance to experiment with how AR can bring static designs to life in a fun and meaningful way.
