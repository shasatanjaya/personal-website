Platform for campus events, competitions, and committees

2023 | UI/UX Design - Figma

CampusEase
is a platform built to make student life easier by gathering all campus events, competitions, and committee opportunities in one place. Instead of digging through posters or scattered announcements, students can simply check CampusEase to see what's happening and how to get involved.

It's all about helping students stay connected, get active on campus, and never miss a chance to join something meaningful.
